Roman Forum

The Roman Forum is located in Plaka, uphill from the Old Market Square,
and it was an open space markets and shops surrounded by columns
on the Roman Empire. Before Roman times the Athenian market was 
a little west. In the market there and the Tower of the Winds, aka "Airs", 
an octagonal building built in the 1st century BC and was used as 
a weather station and clock. 
The Roman Forum is currently only half excavated and visitable.
