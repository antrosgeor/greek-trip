Vergina

If you are interested glorious history of the Macedonians and want to 
go in the space who lived large Macedonian kings including the Great 
Alexander did not have to visit the archaeological site of Vergina which 
is located 63 kilometers from the city of Thessaloniki. All it takes is 
the decision and the time needed for a day trip that will surely reward you.
 Vergina will give you the opportunity to admire the well-preserved tomb 
 of Alexander's father, Philip, and a host of monuments and relics of great 
 artistic value. The area offers a fully organized and modern museum that
 you can visit throughout the year.
